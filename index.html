About the Campaign

The Maritime Awareness Campaign is a nationwide initiative by Naavik to educate and engage communities about the importance of maritime resources, safety, and conservation.

Our mission is to reach students, professionals, and coastal communities across four major regions: Delhi, Lucknow, Bihar, and Mumbai. Through interactive workshops, seminars, and community events, we aim to foster a deeper understanding of our maritime heritage and responsibilities.

This campaign focuses on key areas such as marine conservation, maritime safety practices, career opportunities in the maritime sector, and the economic importance of oceans and waterways to our nation.
